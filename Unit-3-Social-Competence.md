## *Unit-3-Social-Competence*

### *Social-competence*

- *Social competence means having the skills and abilities to handle social situations well.*
- *It involves understanding others' feelings and managing relationships effectively.*
- *These skills are an important part of emotional intelligence, which helps people connect with others and build strong relationships*

---

### *It encompasses two key components*

1. *Relationship-management*
2. *Social-awareness*

---

### *Relationship-Management*

- *Relationship Management means learning how to create and keep good, healthy connections with others.*
- *It includes skills like talking clearly, solving disagreements peacefully, working well with others, and guiding a team effectively.*
- *People who are good at managing relationships can share their thoughts clearly, listen carefully and build strong connections with others.*
- *They can resolve conflicts constructively, find common ground, and negotiate win-win solutions.*
- *Relationship management means working well with others, building trust and encouraging them in a positive way.*

---

### *Social-Awareness*

- *Social awareness means understanding how other people feel, what they need, and seeing things from their point of view.*
- *It means paying attention to how people behave, understanding body language and noticing how people interact with each other.*
- *People who are socially aware can easily understand how others feel and recognize emotions. They are also mindful of different cultures and situations, knowing how to respond appropriately in each context*
- *They have a deep understanding of the impact their words and actions have on others and can adapt their behavior accordingly.*
- *Social awareness means understanding and valuing the differences among people. It includes being inclusive, appreciating diversity and respecting others' opinions and viewpoints.*

---

### *Conclusion*

- *To develop social awareness and good relationship skills, we need to practice, think about our actions, and show a real interest in understanding and connecting with others*
- *This means listening carefully, paying attention to body language and trying to see things from other people's point of view*
- *When we improve these skills we can handle social situations with kindness, build strong and meaningful friendships and make a positive difference in the lives of others*
